# Start Here

This cookbook is not meant to be read front to back like a book.

Use it when you have a concrete teaching task and want AI help that stays grounded in your course.

## How to use this cookbook
1. Start with your task
2. Gather your real materials — your syllabus, assignment, image, or course readings
3. Pick one recipe
4. Paste the recipe prompt into your AI tool with your materials attached
5. Review the result against your actual course context

## Most important rule
Do not rely on zero-shot behavior for important teaching tasks. Always supply your materials.
