This is the first deliverable for the class. As a group, please deliver a 750-1000 word five-paragraph essay that articulates your group’s understanding of your indicated project’s community of benefit and thematic intervention. Please include all citations and bibliography.

Suggested structure:

Paragraph 1 (Introduction) - Introduce your intervention direction and explain how it applies to the local neighborhood. I recommend that you search local news outlets like TapInto New Brunswick, New Brunswick Today, and MyCentralJersey.com for stories about the neighborhood.

Paragraph 2 - Identify the community of benefit for this intervention. By improving quality of life for this intersection, who is being served?

Paragraph 3 - Identify the user audience for this app intervention. Is the community of benefit using this app or is there another audience using this app in support of the community of benefit?

Paragraph 4 - Identify the desired impact or change that you’re hoping to facilitate through the project.

Paragraph 5 (Conclusion) - How are you measuring project efficacy? What might success look like?
