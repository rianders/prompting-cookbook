## Accessibility Standards

| Resource | Link |
|---|---|
| WCAG 2.1 — full specification | [w3.org/TR/WCAG21](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/) |
| WCAG 2.1 — quick reference (how to meet) | [w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/) |
| Section 508 (federal accessibility law) | [section508.gov](https://www.section508.gov/) |

WCAG 2.1+ is the accessibility standard targeted by this cookbook. Section 508 adopts WCAG 2.1 Level AA as its technical standard for federal agencies and recipients of federal funding, which includes most universities.

| Tool | Purpose | Link |

## Privacy and Policy

| Resource | Link |
|---|---|
| FERPA (student privacy) | [studentprivacy.ed.gov](https://studentprivacy.ed.gov/) |
| Rutgers AI policy and guidance | [it.rutgers.edu/ai](https://it.rutgers.edu/ai) |

FERPA prohibits sharing personally identifiable student information with third-party services without appropriate consent or exception. This applies to AI tools — do not paste student names, IDs, grades, or identifying details into any AI prompt.
