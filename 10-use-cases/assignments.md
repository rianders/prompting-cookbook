# Assignments

## Use case
Use AI to convert static assignments into more interactive, scaffolded, or structured learning experiences.

## Good fits
- essay prompts that need staged checkpoints
- reading responses that need more support
- assignments that are too open-ended for the student level
- prompts that need to be translated into Canvas-ready workflows

## What to gather first
- the current assignment prompt
- learning objectives
- rubric or grading criteria
- module readings or course materials
- accessibility, delivery, and hosting constraints

## Design principle
Do not add interactivity just to make something feel more modern. The redesign should preserve the original learning purpose and improve sequence, clarity, or support.

## Watchouts
- do not weaken rigor while increasing scaffolding
- do not add tools or technical requirements that the course cannot support
- do not lose alignment with the rubric or assessment goal
