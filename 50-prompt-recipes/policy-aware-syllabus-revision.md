# Prompt Recipe: Policy-Aware Syllabus Revision

## Goal
Revise or expand a syllabus using institutional guidance, required statements, and local policy sources.

## Use this when
- you already have a syllabus draft

## Gather these materials
- current syllabus draft
- institutional syllabus template
- required accessibility statements
- academic integrity language
- department or campus-specific statements

## Copy/paste prompt
```text
You are helping me revise my course syllabus.

Use the attached materials as the primary source of truth:
- my current syllabus draft
- institutional syllabus template
- required accessibility statements
- academic integrity guidance
- department or campus-specific required statements

Task:
Revise or expand my syllabus so it better reflects the required and recommended guidance while remaining faithful to my course goals.

Important constraints:
- Preserve my course-specific content where possible.
- Do not invent university, school, or department policies that are not present in the materials.
- If a required or recommended element appears to be missing, identify it clearly.

Return:
1. revised syllabus draft
2. list of added or revised policy-related sections
3. checklist of missing or uncertain required elements
4. a short note explaining which supplied sources shaped the revision
```
